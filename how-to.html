VR & How-to

Best Experience

  • VR headset (Quest or similar) — or desktop + mouse to look around.
  • Headphones recommended.
  • Room scale optional — most scenes are comfortable seated.

Navigation

  • Look for hotspots (glowing markers) to move.
  • Short scenes: 1–4 minutes; longer sequences later.
