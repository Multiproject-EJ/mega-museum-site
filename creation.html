Long-Term Plan — 3 Stages

Stage 1 (Now)

3D Comic Animation (Video Series)

A stylized 3D-comic look built from our VR sketches — bold shapes, hand-drawn vibes, quick iteration. Short episodes released here.

  • Design language: graphic silhouettes
  • Rapid animatics → scene polish
  • Community votes drive choices
Stage 2

Stop-Motion Film

Tactile, handmade animation with miniatures and puppets — either via 3D-printed fabrication or commissioned stop-motion artists.

  • Puppet turnarounds & color keys
  • Modular sets & props
  • Frame-by-frame animation, comp & grade
Stage 3

Real Movie (Hybrid Live-Action)

A cinematic, world-scale story blending practical builds, miniatures, and selective VFX. The museum becomes a place you can both visit and watch unfold.

  • VR previz → practical → VFX polish
  • Festival cut + online release
  • Soundtrack & score
