About Mega-Museum

The Mega-Museum is an evolving VR museum built in public — starting with small, honest prototypes. Instead of hiding the mess, we invite people to walk through it with us and help guide what grows next.

Our promise: clarity about what’s live vs. what’s rough; frequent, visible progress; and community votes that influence our next steps.
