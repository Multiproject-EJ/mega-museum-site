About Mega-Museum

The Mega-Museum is an evolving VR museum built in public — starting with small, honest prototypes. Instead of hiding the mess, we invite people to walk through it with us and help guide what grows next.

Our promise: clarity about what’s live vs. what’s rough; frequent, visible progress; and community votes that influence our next steps.

The very first story launches as a vertical, phone-only episode. We storyboard it with StudioOrganize.com, sketch it by hand inside Mental Canvas, and ship the full 25-minute experience for mobile viewers before expanding the world into other formats.

Long-Term Plan — 3 Stages

Stage 1 (Now)

Phone-First Storyboards

Every episode begins as a collaborative storyboard sprint with StudioOrganize.com. Those panels get hand-drawn into a Mental Canvas sequence that plays beautifully on a vertical phone screen.

  • Design language: tall frames, bold silhouettes for mobile viewing
  • 25-minute episodes released exclusively for phones
  • Iterate quickly from storyboard → Mental Canvas → phone preview
Stage 2

Stop-Motion Film

The phone stories evolve into a widescreen, tactile stop-motion production. Miniature sets and clay-forward characters expand the same canon for larger screens.

  • 3D print or sculpt hero puppets and modular sets
  • Shot in true widescreen to celebrate handmade textures
  • Frame-by-frame capture, color grade, and sound design
Stage 3

Immersive VR Museum

The museum itself becomes a VR-native destination. Expect persistent galleries, interactive apps tucked inside exhibits, and a full tour you can walk through in headset.

  • Rooms rebuilt as explorable VR destinations
  • Interactive apps and story moments embedded in the space
  • Guided tours with friends inside the virtual museum

How Patreon Supporters Drive the Plan

  • Community votes lock character looks (clothes, accessories, face structure, names).
  • Polls decide which rooms and set pieces get upgraded next.
  • Suggestions feed gags, transitions, and hidden details across all stages.
